One Regret

Bethania Elira
Romance

PROLOGUE

She lets herself soaked by the heavy rain that night. She thought those memories won’t haunt her anymore, but little did she know that she actually just being denial of what she truly feels?

“This is not me right? This is not what I truly feel, I guess I’m just being more emotional than usual. But why this tears won’t stop? Why I keep on think about it? Why I can’t let go of those musty memories out of my mind?”

Jane Alta, a famous model who loved and adored by a lot of people, decided to retire from the modelling industry right when she reached the peak of her career. She makes everyone shocked by the decision she made because she just made her debut on Paris Fashion Week runway as a newest Prada brand ambassador and got another excellent achievement in her modelling career. People are questioning the actual reason of her retirement due to the lack of explanation she gave on the press conference she held a week after Paris Fashion Week.

ONE WEEK AGO

Jane walking straight to the press conference hall. She’s trying to look as tough as she can showing no hesitation of the decision she’s gonna reveal soon. Jane is someone who once she decide something for herself, she will never regret it or change the decision she has made. The atmosphere in the hall was very quiet, there’s only the sound of journalists' laptop keyboard clicks and the sound of camera shots from reporters and reporters could be heard. Arriving at the hall, Jane walked to her seat and took a deep breath before starting her speech. After a few minutes of silence, Jane breaking the silence by confidently started the speech.

“I had such an amazing times growing up in the modelling industry for almost 7 years. This has become my dream since I was still a kid and it’s not a one night success to be in this position. It costs blood, sweet, and tears in every way I’ve been through of becoming a model. I also feel so grateful for all the love and support that people give, it means so much to me. However, after a lot of consideration and contemplation I had, I decided to retire from being a model and continue my life in another path I chose. I apologize for this sudden announcement I made and thank you very much for all the overflowing love and support for me for this past 7 years.”

The statement Jane said on the press conference of her sudden retirement left everyone in awe. She didn’t said any specific reason on her statement which brings a lot of people speculating on the actual reasons. A scandal even emerges to a surface, love affair with a basketball athlete, Greg Ashton. An unknown stranger uploaded a blurry pic of Jane and Greg on their social media account and said that picture was taken right a week before the press conference was held. This news become viral but Jane remains silent.

PRESENT TIME

“Ah, they finally know it”, said Jane while watching the news about her on television.

“Well I dont live in a spotlight anymore, I dont have anything that I have to protect this time”, said her then she turns off the television right away. She opens the door to her balcony and sit on her favorite chair. There are 2 chairs she puts in her balcony. She sits while giving an empty gaze to the empty chair next to her. She suddenly has no thoughts and shocked by the strange thing she sees in that chair. It is a small jewelry box. She takes that box and opens it, there is a necklace with a little lock pendant, and a neatly folded letter. She gently opens the letter and read it. There is only one sentence written in that small letter, “let’s be together forever, Jane Alta.” and tears started to streaming down from her eyes. Every memories she had that she painstakingly buried suddenly raged in her mind and bring her to the bittersweet past she had.

FLASHBACK

“Do you think the world will end tomorrow?”, asked Greg

“I hope so,” said Jane

The first conversation Jane had with Greg suddenly haunts Jane’s mind. Both Greg and Jane met when they were at the lowest point in their life. Jane was in front of the convenience store, waiting for her mother who promised to spend new year eve together with Jane, but even until the year changed, she didn’t show up. Jane is a brokenhome daughter who lives with her grandmother, her father died when she was still 5 and her mother is always busy with her business. Greg was drunk on their first meeting, he was drinking alone after being suspended from his basketball team due to his low performance at that time which made his team kept on losing.

Jane was only 18 and Greg was only 20 when they first met. They barely have friends at that time which made them can only leaned on each other and being each other’s support system. They had every painful and joyful moments in their life shared with each other. They always accompany each other for years they spent when they are working hard to achieve each others’ dreams. After years being each other’s support system and slowly achieving more in the process of reaching their dreams, a lot of unexpected things happened.

After long time knowing and always being on each other’s side, when Jane was 22 and Greg 24 years old, they decided to date. One year of their relationship was beautiful, they rarely fight and argue, even when they have a little fight, it won’t lasts more than a day. But sometimes life doesn't always go according to our plans. At the age of 25, Greg was recruited to be the part of International Basketball Team which made him must have training and live in United States. He must leave Jane in Singapore alone. Both of them were totally fine with a long distance relationship. They believe distance is not a big deal for their relationship as they’ve been through more complicated things before.

But everything can change, so does human. Greg had a hard time during his training in U.S, that is the hardest training he has done in his life. It’s not only about the practice and exercise to become the part of International Basketball Team but the seniority and racism are also extreme especially to the Asian people. Greg as a newbie and an Asian as well have to face many difficulties with the seniors, they often ask him to lend them money, force him to fulfil their favor, and many other ridiculous things.

However, Greg thought he can’t tell those struggle he had there to Jane since he didn’t want her to worry about him when he clearly knew she also have to face a lot of difficulties on her career. But Greg didn’t realize that he didn’t being honest to Jane is actually a bad thing for their relationship. Unwittingly, he rarely contacts Jane, and even when Jane tried to contact him first, he often didn’t answer the call nor reply the message from Jane. Both of them were having a hard time due to the lack of communication.

Jane, a person who always makes a quick decision, thought if maybe their relationship just can’t work anymore. Greg who keeps on having a tougher time in the U.S thought that he will just gonna tell Jane everything after a year when his training is done and can meet Jane in person.

After a year has passed, Greg came back to Singapore and her first destination is Jane’s place. When he arrived, she’s not around. Because it is still awkward between them, Greg called her manager about Jane. That is the moment where he realized, everything is already messed up because of him. He didn’t even know Jane was in Paris at that time for her debut on Paris Fashion Week. He cried realizing how hard it must be for Jane during this past one year. Later on Greg decided to flew to Paris hoping he can fix their relationship. When he left, Greg didn’t realize that he left the gift he wanted to give to Jane in the chair on Jane’s apartment balcony.

Upon his arrival in Jane’s place in Paris, the atmosphere is extremely awkward between both of them.“H-how did you know I’m in Paris and specifically in this hotel?”, said Jane in a slightly angry tone. Then she looked at her manager next to her who looking down with worried face and she just knew right away what happened. Greg walked up to jane and hugged her tightly while whispering to her ear, “I miss you and I’m sorry, Jane”. Jane, who was already disappointed with Greg's behavior the past year, slowly let go of Greg's hug. “I have to go, let’s talk about that later” said Jane in a calm tone.

Jane was actually wanted to travel around Paris the time when Greg came, but she decided to just left Paris that day and flew back to Singapore. This time Jane’s manager can’t tell anything to Greg anymore. Greg can’t find any information about Jane anymore so he decided to left Paris as well.

As soon as Greg arrived in Paris, he straight up went to Jane’s place. He saw Jane cries her balcony while hugging herself. Jane who realized Greg came, suddenly feel the urge to let her emotion out that time. She asked him bitterly, “Oh I thought you forget that I exist, don’t you?”, Greg answered in a calm voice, “Jane please let me explain it first,”, Jane laugh like disdain then said, “Explain it? What exactly do you want to explain? You found another girl there? You living your best life there so you don’t even time to call me or even text me?”. Right when she said it, Greg can’t help but tell everything to her in an angry and sad tone. Unfortunately, those honesty from Greg just makes everything become worse, it just makes Jane becomes more convinced of the decision she made, which is to end their relationship. She does think it is not the distance that ruined their relationship but she had this feeling from the time Greg hard to communicate that Greg is not the same anymore. Back then everytime Greg having a hard time, he always tell Jane about anything, he shares the burden he felt to Jane but when he is happy, he also share it with Jane. Jane thinks it will not be the same anymore if Greg’s mindset of their relationship has changed.

“What makes you think it is right to tell me now and not the time when it becomes a burden to you? Greg I’m sorry but it just convince me that I don’t have to spend more time to contemplating about our relationship, it just won’t work out between you and me anymore. Before you always share everything with me, both your hard times and good times. But now, you only want to share the good times with me, and I don’t want it Jack. I don’t want that kind of relationship between us,” said Jane.

Jane's words make Greg speechless. Greg knows what kind of person Jane is that once she made a decision for her life, she won’t regret it or take her words back.

Greg left her without saying anything on that day. After Greg left, Jane cried non stop. She doesn’t believe it really has to end up like that. There’s also one thing that she has been contemplate about since their relationship started to fell apart, her relationship with Greg is different, it is more than just a ‘lovey dovey’ relationship, it’s all about the process of her pursuing her dreams and becomes a famous model she is right now. She almost give up on her dreams and on her life when she met him. But meeting him felt like starting a new life for Jane. Separating with him felt like her whole world falling apart. Jane decided to started a completely new life again and left everything behind, including her dream job as a model.

PRESENT TIMES

Unwittingly, she lets herself walk in the street while holding that little box from Greg. She keeps on walking until the rain start falling and she realized she’s already in the front of the convenience store, the place where she and Greg first met. Suddenly tears streaming down on her face. For the first time in her life she feels like she has made a wrong decision. For the first time in her life she wishes she could go back to the time she end it all with Greg and take back every words she said.
