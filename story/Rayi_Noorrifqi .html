Hasta La Vista

Rosdiana Sahara
Romance

My name is Cecillion Northwind, a half-elf that lives in the village of the north mountain of Caelid. This village is guarded by the knights of Caelid, including myself, a commander in the battalion with my father. We protect the village from the evil forces that started appearing from the darkness a few years ago. We have fought the evil forces for a few years while finding the causes of how the shadow fiend started appearing in north Caelid mountain. We started tracking for about a year, and we have finally found the source of the appearance of the dark fiend. One night my father started a search mission without me to join his troops and headed to the cave near Caelid by himself. He said that the mission would only take two weeks to finish because he invited the best knights at that time to make sure the evil forces were wiped out as soon as possible.

Two weeks went by, no report from him or his army. Because I had a bad feeling about this, I went there with a small team available and went there alone to make sure that my father was okay. The trip was quite difficult. There were enemies around. A giant bug that drinks people blood dry leaves them with their skin and bones. There were war-skilled dark knights, commanders with lots in number, making our kingdom lost in the battlefield against them, and more. My team was able to arrive at the cave safely. Upon arriving there, I could not find any trace of my father's team when I looked around the cave.

“Don’t worry, sir, I’m sure that your father is around here somewhere since from the report that I have read, this cave is huge,” said one of my friends who is the scout in my party.

“I hope you’re right, but first, let’s make sure that we are safe around here and check the perimeter, and let’s rest for today,” I answered my question.

        My team then started to check the perimeter and set up camp for the night before we started to head and search for the cave in the morning. We made sure to check and block the entrance and set up a magic barrier around our camp to keep us safe.

“The barrier and the entrance have been secured, sir.”

“Thank you,” I replied, then we went ahead and went to sleep for the night.

        In the morning, we heard some explosions inside the cave deeper. I immediately picked up my weapon and entered a fighting stance. Everyone was at the edge and waiting to launch an attack if there was an ambush. We were waiting for a minute, but nothing came our way. I then commanded everyone to pack their bag and get ready to go deeper inside the cave. After a few minutes of walking, we heard someone laughing inside one of the tunnels that lead to a large cave area where everything was dark with only a candle that lit up in the middle of the place and a man laughing maniacally wearing a black robe while chanting ancient words.

“Who are you? And what are you doing here?” I asked the man with a black hood.

Suddenly he stopped and started to look in our direction. Bloodshot eyes were the first thing that I noticed when the man looked at me. Suddenly we felt a bloodthirst filling up the air. We immediately prepared our weapons, and the mage started to cast a protection spell to shield us from the attack that the mysterious man was about to release. But it was too late since the magic force that the mysterious person in the black hood was too fast and wiped out half of my squad on my right. I couldn’t understand what happened and when I looked to my right, blood splattered across the wall, with the guts falling soon after from above.

“hahaha…. HAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!!!!!! The shock I see. What do you feel right now? Afraid, angry, or, let me guess, nothing. Since that happened too quickly.” He smiled at us.

I clenched my teeth and asked again, “who…. are you?!” While I pointed my swords to his face.

“The name Axaroth, but it doesn’t matter since that’s the only thing that you should know.” 

        I could feel a rumble suddenly appear right under my feet, and suddenly, a roar was heard. I was immediately jumped and roled to the right. Suddenly a giant leviathan eats all of my remaining troops and my friend. In rage, I swung my sword to the leviathan eyes only to leave a small cut in its eye. The creature fled again underground to go in the other direction. I then heard claps by Axaroth,

“Fascinating,” he said to me.

He then removes the hood from his head, and I can see a pure black color on his hair and a sinister smile coming from his face.

“Don’t tell me, are you, by any chance, the child of the commander that came here a few weeks ago?”

“The hair, the sword, it makes sense now.”

        I clenched my sword and quickly slashed my sword to his neck, but my blade couldn't seem to cut it because of the dark force field that kept my sword from cutting him. I took a step back and focused on my magic to put it in my sword and set it on fire.

“FLAME BLADE!!” 

I rushed to Axaroth as fast as I could and swiftly placed one sword to his head and the other one on his waist. When my swords were about to touch him, he jumped right in the middle of my swords, dodged both slabs, but one of my swords managed to cut a little bit of his hair. He then proceeded to cast a spell in an ancient language. I then suddenly pushed back by a magical force, but I managed to hold my ground by stabbing the ground.

“Ha-ha… interesting, now let’s get serio-,“ he stopped in the middle of the sentence, followed by the disappearance of his smile from his face. He then looked up, bothered. The look in his eyes was like someone was talking to him through telepathy, delivering bad news.

“Tck, we shall continue next time, Cecillion.”

He disappeared into thin air before I managed to ask him a question.

“Stop, what did you do to my father?!!!!!”

And then Silence was the only thing that filled that place, and not long after, I heard someone chanting in the distance, followed by a rumble in the ground.

“Eldritch Blast”

I followed the source of the sound. It led to a place filled with water and only a platform where a party of 5 stood. The party consisted of 2 men, one of them seeming to be the warlock that cast an eldritch blast earlier. The other one was a person who was holding a small lyre. There was a triton that cast spells to guard their surroundings that seemed like a cleric of the group. Next was a small girl holding a staff next to the bard that was hiding behind his back for protection. An Aarakocra, a humanoid creature with a face of a bird with a human body. The party was facing the large leviathan that attacked me earlier.

“Ophi, can you like to fly us out of here?!” said the bard to the Aarakocra.

“How can I fly when one of my wings is broken?” He replied.

        The leviathan then attacked but failed because of the force field the triton cleric made earlier successfully cast and blocked the leviathan attack.

“Guys, focus and try to find its weakness instead of mumbling around, back there,” said the cleric.

“LOOK OUT,” said the warlock when the leviathan suddenly whipped its tail behind the party and knocked the girl to fall from the platform.

“JOSSELYN!!!!” The bard screamed. The Aarakocra then jumped into the water to save the girl from drowning. He managed to retrieve the girl to the surface. Then the leviathan changed its target to them, who were vulnerable in the water.

        I must help. I rushed and jumped as far as I could, using my agility as an elf to land a strike on the leviathan's face. Managed to have the leviathan's attention, to kept it away from the bird guy and the girl.

“Who are you?” asked the warlock.

“No time to explain. Now can you use another spell to blast the platform above us? Which is a sharp rock over there?” I ask the warlock.

“Sure, ELDRITCH BLAST.”

He then fired his spell, managed to collapse the rock above us and made the sharp rock pierced the leviathan body holding it in place.

The cleric used an area forced field spell to cover us from the other debris to guard us. I then focused the magic energy on my swords again and light them up on fire.

“FLAME BLADE!”

Before I rushed to attack, the bard cast a spell on me to grant me extra strength for my attack.

“STRENGTHEN.”

        I then jumped to the leviathan's head. Managed to cut his head open but was unable to kill it just yet because the cut was not deep enough.

Then the girl shouted, “Eldritch Blast!” 

﻿She finished up the leviathan. We took a breath after defeating such beast, then the male warlock asked again.

“Who are you?”

“The name’s Cecillion Northwind, and I’m here to help you.”
