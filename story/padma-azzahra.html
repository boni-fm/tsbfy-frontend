Rain on A Summer Day

Padma Azzahra
Romance

The rain was unable to stop on that day in December. People hated the sudden and heavy rain since they were occupying themselves in order to avoid getting drenched. Her entire life she had always adored the rain dearly. Indeed, the rain is possibly falling every day, but is it wrong if someone loves the rain? Is it wrong to fall in love with someone?

She was trying to get drenched by swaying and dancing under the rain. Not long ago, there was a tall man in a black coat covering his formal attire, approaching her.

“I am more afraid if you getting cold afterward, then I might have to take care of you, my love,” said the man under the umbrella,

“Then, let it be. I was waiting for this in my dearest life after long summer, young man” she chuckled knowing her man was worried about her,

“Are you perhaps so grateful about the rain or my proposal, young lady?”

“Perhaps, maybe” she teased,

Then, he allowed his woman to celebrate their special day, a special day that she accepted his permission to step ahead in their relationship. He smiled ear-to-ear because of her answers most of the time that only God knew when he was able to know how to stop smiling. People might think he was a mad yet foolish man, yes, he was madly in love with her. 

“I think it’s enough for your little dance since you have caught attention from everyone around here. Also, I assure you will get sick” he warned by pulling her waist,

“But now you get wet” he teased her,

(giggle) “Okay, okay, my Dear. I obey your order only for this time. Are you willing to compensate for ruining my little euphoria here?”

“Well, I do. Let’s get you home first.”

The man covered her soaked clothes with her dark-long coat that he prepared in his car. They walked out of the street and playfully told how foolish they were on this day. Holding hands like their life depended on it and kept walking towards the man’s car. He firstly prioritised his woman by opening the passenger seat door in terms of getting in the car first, followed by him and starting his car. The whole ride was calm and steady along with playing the woman’s favourite playlist.

“I will never be bored of this song because it reminds me when we first met” she said,

“I doubt that since I didn’t hear the same song with you. At that time, I was captivated at your beauty”

“Geez… that is the cheesiest thing that I’ve ever heard”

“I was just teasing! Seriously, I didn't even know that before you told me in our first year. You were only busy with your errands, listening to the song and writing something”

The man focused back on his track. Later on, the rain had just stopped and helped his vision to see clearly on the road. By that time their first destination was already near, and he had to stop his car in front of her house. His plan was not finished, she got more surprised with a painting of her that he made himself. A beautiful painting with dried daisies as her gown dress. The painting was on behalf of his compensation while he already intended for the longest time. As well she was soon to become a lovely bride for him. The woman kissed his hand and left his car, then waved her hand in signalling goodbye to him. He answered her goodbye and continued his driving to reach his final track.

Along the way to his own house, he mesmerised by the memories that he had experienced with her, the person that he dearly cherished. She might not be his first love but somehow, she was able to create everything as his first. For almost six years being in a relationship with her was the ethereal thing that he had had. She loved the rain as well as she was the rain to the arid of himself. It was enough to convince him that she was the only one by proposing to her on Christmas Day under the mistletoe. Since he secretly read all of her wishlists through her journal. She was everything that he has been seeking for, he thought.

Day to day, night to night, as the roses began to bloom. It seems that the roses were extremely acknowledged in which time to grow. The sun gently gave a great morning to the folks who began their own errands. A piece of music played along in her bedroom while she concentrated on styling her hair and wearing a Florence waterfall dress in sage green satin. Oh, she desperately wished that her man would have known that she had cried a lot over a letter that she already regretted opening and their trivial argument last night. People might be saying it was a trial for the soon-to-be wedded couple. Nevertheless, they tried to communicate with each other in order to prevent something bad happening to them. A sudden bell rang from the outside that was interrupting her little thoughts, indicating her man had just arrived.

“How do I look? Is it too much or too basic?”

“You still look gorgeous either way. How’s the medical result of your blood test?”

“Oh, they informed me that they will send it tomorrow morning”

“Alright, are you ready?”

“Almost. I would like to check my makeup first”

“Perfect, as always. But before arriving at my cousin's wedding, we would like to stop by our future house first.”

“Sure thing, I am very pleased to see our house”

She smiled with full force to convince her man that she was alright. A soft yet quick peck from her fiancé and softly whispering “I’m sorry for last night” was enough for her. He knew better than the heaven up there of the thoughts that slipped in his fiancée’s mind, according to him. The man implied to go down from her house and drove away to the house which would belong to them.

The tree leaves abruptly fell down all around the pedestrian street. The fallen leaves hindered the drivers’ views as it fell on the windshield. A car parked in front of an old house. Quite damaged and a little bit abandoned since there were barely any tenants that had stayed in the past. Seeing from the roof, it looked like it would fall at any time. On the other side, each side of the walls still strongly stood to hold the things they left. Suddenly, the man and woman were warmly greeted by an old lady who wore a comfortable shirt and a black floral-patterned skirt. It was the landlady of the house who allowed them to get in the house.

“I am sorry for this messy house. I sell this house because I want to see a fresh and new life here”

(smile) “It’s alright, Sophia. Your wish is similar to ours since we would like to do something new here. Isn't it, my love?” The man asked her fiancée,

“This old house once was a madhouse, so that’s why all of the stuffs are chaotic” Sophia continued informing along with her warm smile,

(titter) “Well, I think there’s no need to renovate this, my dear. It’s perfectly made for me” she answered with a half-suppressed laugh,

Because of that, they remained silent for a couple of minutes.

“Well, I think we have had enough to see everything here. I assure you that we are a little bit interested in this old house. Thank you, Sophia”

“I should be the one who thanks you, young man.”

They stepped out of the house with carefully yet strangely steps. Not a single word came out from their mouth to break the silence between her and him. The woman only turned her head to look at the road as they were on the way to the place where his cousin married. It was a habit for him to open the passenger seat door and gently request her soft hand. They met many relatives and friends of the man. It was a slight reunion for them, especially since they introduced themselves as fiancé and fiancée this time. In a moment, the bride was smiling brightly and holding her bridal bouquet as everyone in the hall was waiting for this little tradition. The bride had tossed her bouquet which surprisingly was caught by the man. The unexpected moment he was having made him grab his woman in no time. Everyone seemed to have thought that they caught the bouquet together following the cheers and clapping sounds from everyone for that moment.

After a while, the nuances of many romantic songs thoroughly filled the hall which delighted those who danced with their lovers. His eyes dilated as he looked at her brown eyes, suddenly he pulled her closer taking the lead by holding her waist tightly. She eased her weight so she could dance comfortably. The way their forehead leaned on each other and closed their eyes in drowning their meaningful dance.

“I can’t wait to worship you more in the future after we pronounce ourselves as husband and wife. Then, we both slowly have wrinkles in our face, then grey hair…” The Man said and still closing his eyes,

“Then, we watch our kids with their kids from afar” he continued his sentence and slowly disconnected his forehead,

His fiancée lifted her head and deeply looked into his eyes. Her eyes were trembling and her mouth slightly parted as she was about to utter some words. His eyes first filled with hope and happiness, now immediately filled with confusion and lost. Nonetheless, there were no words coming out of her mouth, then she abruptly left him dumbfounded. She excused herself from the dance party. The man swiftly followed her rushing steps and tried to shout her name and block her step at every chance.

“Darling, what on earth are you doing? You acted so strangely today! Are you probably mad at me from last night?” The Man raised his voice,

“If it annoys you, then I’m sorry! And no, this isn’t about last night”

“Then, please don’t behave like this!”

“Fine! You said you want to have kids. Well, I’m sorry about that because I don’t!”

“We can adopt a child! There’s something that happened to you. Would you be honest with me? Look into my eyes”

In the midst of reticence and desolation, whether was it their own feelings or the situation they were in? No one was able to explain or answer this awkward situation. Hiding the melancholy from herself, she left him dumbfounded, again. He wished to move his feet, stop her and scream until his lungs were out of him, yet he was unable to think of what happened to him.

The clock was ticking and the hand-kept running as life was still going on. Two months after the incident, there were no messages, calls, or even mails from her. People might be thinking that he was far from being a gentleman while he was trying to put himself first. On the other side, the darkness and loneliness surrounded and ate all of herself since her tears flowed down endlessly. This is so much worse; this was far as they wanted. She decided to get up her body forcefully and wrote a letter to him. Contemplating what kind of words she needed to explain what had happened to her and him. She walked down and went to his house, yet she acknowledged that she had no rights to meet him this soon. Sensing a heightened adrenaline rush into her blood, she put her letter in front of his house. Before she met him, she ran away as far as she could do her best.

In the evening, the man came home from work and found a letter written by his lover. Meet me at 8 in the place where we first became engaged. He wondered where it was, was it the place when they first met? Was it the place when they first dated? What did she mean they got engaged? He had no time to figure out the place since his clock showed 6 p.m. which meant that he had to rush. Assuming the meaning of engaged, he assures that it was the place where he proposed to her. She cherished the dusk of the sun because she thought that the sun was saying goodbye and promising to be back soon. For this reason, he enlightened himself to believe that he was right. Indeed, he knew all too well everything about her. He found her as she also was waiting for his presence. The luminance from the lamp defined her deep and porcelain eyes and made his heart pounding so fast. She found him too after a while of waiting and hoping he would come. He began taking several steps to her table, yet still, they kept silent for a couple of minutes.

(chuckled) “You absolutely know everything about me.” the woman smiled faintly,

“I am so embarrassed right now because of that night. My energy keeps draining so much, and I didn’t know how to contact you properly. So, I guess… here we are. You’re still wearing my favourite perfume; it smells good from here”

“Indeed, I do. It was your present, right?”

“Surely, I do… Do you remember when you took a blood test before me, and you were diagnosed with thalassemia minor?” the woman straightly expressed her intention,

“Yes, I do. What’s the matter of that? I am still doing good”

“No, not that… that is the reason why I behaved like this is that I have it too”

The heavy wind swayed and left chills through his skin. He suddenly stopped and became completely still and quiet after he heard her statement clearly. The sight of her vulnerable and weak state that made the deepest part of him screaming.

“Then… will there be no us?”

“It will always be there for us. I do to want to worship you more in the future, yet we pronounced ourselves as a friend” she started sobbing and she tried to wipe her tears,

“My love... Oh, I’m sorry because I used to call you that. I sincerely apologise for everything that I have done to you. Never in a million years, I have an intention to hurt you. But, with this reason, we have to live on our own, not as partners but as a person. So, promise me one thing. Never feel guilty about moving on as I wish you to be happier than ever with someone else rather than seeing you bear with sorrow yet alone. Please?” she continued,

“I can’t guarantee that I will be happier with someone else that is not you. We can still continue this until we get married, then adopt a child!”

“No, it’s too risky for both of us. Please, there’s no other solution for us”

“There is no solution to us” he repeated her words with full emphasized,

She took off her engagement ring and returned it to its owner by excluding herself that was no longer his. She pulled him closer for the last time as well as her lips lastly met his lips because it was their promise every time they had an argument. She went out in absence herself in that place and also her existence in his life. His surroundings made him lose his balance and about to fall. Excusing himself from that place, he lazily walked to his house. Then, it suddenly started raining at the very wrong time. He forgot to take the umbrella since he was in the rush before. This heavy rain was unable to wash the pain from his heart. He thought that he found the right puzzle for his life yet the universe was a gigantic puzzle most of the time. A couple celebrated for the sudden rain which caught his eyes, the two of them were dancing and letting themselves get drenched. Besides that, there was no one to voluntarily celebrate for him except the rain.
