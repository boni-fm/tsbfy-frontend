Hasta La Vista

Rosdiana Sahara
Romance

This is the story of a boy who chased the sunset until it chased him

One day, there’s a boy named Pierre, he lives in a little town on the southside of Christchurch, New Zealand. He never quite sure about his placement in this world, he’s confused, lost, broken, invisible.

He’s not even sure about himself or where he’s been in this blue world.

One day, Pierre met a girl, his name is Abigaelle, he thought she has the prettiest name that he ever heard. Abigaelle is a pretty girl who lives on the Christchurch’s northern outskirts.

Pierre enjoys chasing sunsets, so he travels around town from the southern to the northern parts of Christchurch, and from the eastern to the western parts of Christchurch, all to see the beautiful sunsets that are usually seen in the northern part of Christchurch.

He went there frequently just to see the most beautiful sunset and Abigaelle. Abigaelle, on the other hand, is a girl who enjoys chasing sunsets, but since the most beautiful sunset is located near where she lives, she doesn't have to chase it as much as Pierre does.

Every day, Abigaelle sits on her balcony to watch the sunset; she lives in a cottage near the beach, where Pierre also goes to watch the sunset.

She then went to the beach to observe the sunset. She just realized that watching it from the beach looks much better because it appears to be closer. Pierre then approached Abigaelle as she sat on the sand watching the sunset. Abigaelle initially ignores him, but eventually Pierre melts her with his poetic words, and she realizes that this man can speak in poems.

They began to talk about nature and the town's beautiful environment, exchanging ideas and perspectives on people from the south and north.

In this case, people from the southside usually don't like the way people from the northside live; they're usually jealous because people from the northside usually live individually and most of them live in a glamorous and comfortable way.

In contrast, the people of the South are used to struggling to find their own happiness, and the majority of them live in the most complex way possible.

Abigaelle admires Pierre's way of thinking and his perspective on northern people, as he stated, "Northern people are the people who have been struggling and working hard in order to achieve their dreams, and I look up to them so much."

Time flies, the two have been meeting frequently to watch sunsets and talk, Pierrre then fell in love with Abigaelle, "She's extremely adorable," he said the first time he saw her.

Abigaelle knows a lot about Pierre and what he did in the past, she knows his complicated life and tragic past.

Pierre was a boy who’s been through a lot of ups and downs but never managed to help himself to get out of it. He thought Abigaelle could help himself to heal.

They get along, but it’s only for a while.

Months after they get along, Pierre seems to get better and better with his life.

“It feels like I can’t live without you, Abi” he said that to Abigaelle.

“I can’t remember everything, how I lived before you came into my life” says Pierre.

Pierre knew that he had loved and had been loved, but he had no chronological order to place it.

He had no idea how to confess his feelings to Abi, afraid that she might not want him to be with her.

Time flies, and Abigaelle never forgets what Pierre's life was like in the past; she's afraid of becoming attached to him one day. So, she decided to abandon him because she believes their relationship will never work.
