Yes, It's You

Zaki Narayan Satria
Romance

Love is one of the important things in everyone's life. Love can be directed to anyone in our lives. Parents, friends, pets, and also someone who will be a friend for life until the end of life. When feelings of love arise from both sides of the human being, it can create a very close relationship. But if the feeling of love is only one-sided, it can be very painful and traumatic for some people. Yes, you could say unrequited love. Maybe not many have experienced it, but for some people, unrequited love can be a pretty deep trauma and can make the person afraid to fall in love again after that. Just like what happened to Zach. Just like what happened to Zach. Unrequited love is a new thing in Zach's life. He is a good man, patient, loving, and never rude to those he loves. Zach is also a humble person and always puts the interests of others first. Unrequited love is a new problem for Zach, who just separated from his ex-girlfriend a few months ago. Since Zach separated from his ex-girlfriend, he often talks to his best friend named Anne. Anne is a small adorable woman, with slanted eyes and shoulder-length hair. Zach doesn't have any feelings for Anne. Zach really thinks Anne is his best friend because they often exchange stories with each other about many things. Such as education, work, family, and others. Anne has always been a good listener to Zach, and vice versa. Zach talks more about his love life, and Anne talks more about the worries in her life.

Anne is a cheerful woman and has a very positive aura when around her. She is also very loving to her parents, and very good at controlling herself when in trouble. Zach doesn't talk to Anne regularly enough, only on a few occasions. Zach also doesn't feel love for Anne at all. If so, then why did Zach suddenly fall in love with Anne? Even though Anne is not trying to make Zach comfortable, just being a good listener. In a few moments, Zach had a feeling "Is she the right person?" against Anne. The cause may be fairly common. Together with Anne, Zach feels more valued as a man. Anne also sometimes likens herself to be Zach's girlfriend in some of the stories that Zach tells. For example, when Zach tells about his ex-girlfriend who doesn't appreciate Zach's efforts and throws harsh words that hurt Zach. Anne also argued, "If I were your girlfriend, maybe I wouldn't do that. That's definitely not a natural trait of a woman. She shouldn't be swallowed up by her own ego and cornered you, Zach." Opinions like that several times make Zach blush and bring a little feeling of falling in love with Anne. The way he listens to Anne, who is very patient and always aware of Zach's position in his relationship, is also one aspect of why Zach fell in love with Anne. Zach had also met with Anne several times and exchanged stories about their life. This meeting makes Zach also quite fall in love with Anne because there are many things that trigger it. The gestures that Anne made to Zach several times made Zach blush and feel awkward. For example, Anne said, "Never mind, Zach, you are a good person, you don't deserve to be treated like that" while stroking and lightly patting Zach's shoulder. Anne also laughed out loud a few times while hitting Zach's shoulder or hand with exasperation. Which made Zach blush again deep down in his heart.

Basically, apart from Anne's way of thinking that caught Zach's attention, Anne's gestures and words made Zach blush a lot and start to fall in love with Anne. Zach is almost interested in all aspects of Anne. He likes women who have short hair, likes women who are cheerful, and likes women who are loving and not easily provoked by emotions. All of that is in Anne.

But one thing that holds Zach from expressing his feelings is, that Anne is just his best friend, who probably can't have a relationship more than that. It's also possible that Anne doesn't have the same feelings as Zach. It also becomes a barrier for Zach to express his feelings. Zach really wants to express his feelings, but Zach is afraid of telling the truth, Anne becomes distant from Zach, and may not be friends anymore. Zach really doesn't want to lose Anne. Zach once cried at certain moments just thinking about what he should do with Anne. Do you have to express your feelings or just be quiet and wait for time? Especially with Zach who hasn't finished with his past with his ex, Zach of course has doubts about his feelings. He was afraid to only make Anne an outlet for his heartache. At one point, Zach was contemplating his feelings, aligning his mind and heart to match the question within himself, "Am I really in love with Anne, or is it just temporary?".

There was one moment when Zach was sure of himself and his feelings. When Zach met Anne and his other friends, then Zach took Anne home on a motorbike, and Zach really felt a very deep love that made Zach's heart beat fast and made goosebumps. When Anne talks to Zach on the motorbike, Zach feels comfortable. Zach also couldn't contain himself when Anne grabbed his hand to say goodbye. Anne said, "Be careful on the road, I hope you stay happy always", while holding Zach's hand for a long time plus looking into Zach's eyes. Indeed it was just an act of saying goodbye. It's different for Zach, what makes it possible is his best moment with Anne which makes Zach unable to contain his embarrassment and admiration for Anne. At that moment, which took place at the cafe, Zach took several photos with Anne. The photo is still often looked at by Zach every night. Zach could only stare at the photo and hope for a miracle to come. There was a moment when Zach was awakened by his friend who said, "What if Anne suddenly tells you about a boy who wants to be with her, and Anne is also attracted to him? You don't want that moment to happen when you still have the same feelings for her?”. At that moment Zach He is considering whether he should express his feelings to Anne by risking his friendship status which might make Zach lose Anne because Anne is away from him, or maybe Zach will get a positive response because Anne has the same feelings, but Zach always thinks the worst first.

Until now, Zach could only admire Anne from a distance. And can only dream of having a future life with Anne. Zach is trapped in his own insecurity as well. Zach feels unworthy of Anne. Zach felt he was not handsome enough for a beautiful woman like Anne. What would Anne's friends say if she had a boyfriend who wasn't handsome and unattractive? Zach also feels ashamed of himself for not doing enough in academics. Meanwhile, Anne is very studious and is always on time for her assignments. The material aspect is also something that makes Zach feel inappropriate for Anne. Zach is not from a rich family. Zach doesn't feel like he can give Anne much materialist stuff. Zach only capitalized on love and sincerity. Zach still often sees his photos with Anne. And still often see Anne's Instagram. Admiring how sweet and cute Anne is at night is Zach's activity at times. The risk that Zach bears is quite large. He didn't want to lose Anne's trust. He didn't want to be shunned by Anne. But he's also afraid that Anne's relationship with someone else will suddenly break Zach's heart. Zach could only bury his feelings deep inside. Refrain from telling Anne.

In the end, Zach can only let go of his feelings if Anne has a boyfriend in the future. Zach can only wish Anne the best for now. Hope Anne can do her best for her studies, and for her work. Zach doesn't want to bother Anne by expressing his feelings, only adding to Anne's burden. Maybe one day Zach will tell Anne about his feelings, whatever the risk. Because that feeling is very stuck in Zach's heart. Zach must be sincere with any answer Anne. Zach will probably be waiting for Anne for an undetermined amount of time. But clearly, Zach is very in love with Anne, and Zach hopes Anne has the same feelings as him. Zach felt this might be unrequited love for him.
