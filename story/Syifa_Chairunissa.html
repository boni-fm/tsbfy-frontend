Hasta La Vista

Rosdiana Sahara
Romance

Glancing at the brand new watch on her wrist, it already says seven thirty two P.M. Yet she is still sitting on the chair inside this place, facing the only computer here. Inhaling deeply, she could smell the freshness left from today’s stock still dancing in the air. She smiled as her left hand tucked the hair behind her ear and started to make some notes. In her head, she remembered buying this small building two years ago right after she graduated from college. She knows it sounds crazy to start a business without many friends beside her, but with some help from her very supportive childhood friend, she made this place into a small flower shop. This childhood friend of hers loves to give a hand here every weekend just like what he did today.

"Hey," someone said while she heard the footsteps approaching her slowly.

"Hi," She dropped her pen as she shot her head up to see her childhood friend. "Wait, I thought you were home already?"

"Yeah, I was," he walked and stopped in front of the desk, next to some lilies and tulips.

"Then.. what are you doing here?" Furrowing her eyebrows as she observes him carefully. She can feel her heart beating a little bit faster.

"To give you this" and then he dropped a paper bag containing a box inside it on the desk, she leaned her body to see what’s inside immediately.

"What is that?" She looks nervous when she touches the paper bag. But then she takes it anyway. Her childhood friend giggled at her response.

"Oh come on. Do you really think I'm capable of giving you something awful?"

"Well, you do like to make fun of me sometimes, so of course you are capable of giving me something awful just to see me miserable" she stops examining the paperbag and stares at him.

"Ouch. I'm not that evil. And I only make fun of you because I love you, Daisy" he laughed and hit her shoulder playfully to try to make it sound like a joke.

The truth is, he really does. And not the kind of love he gives to his other friends, like he always said to them all this time.

She doesn't respond to what he says. Instead, she pulls out the box that has been inside the paper bag.

"What is this?" She looks at him.

He rolled his eyes, "Can't you just open it and figure it out by yourself? Come on, I promise that one is not a joke"

She pouts at his reply but proceeds to open the box anyway. She seems stunned when she finally figures out what's inside the box. There are 7 cupcakes with a note at the top of the box, saying 'Happy Birthday, Daisy Cooper'.

"Happy birthday, Daisy. I know you think I ain’t care that much about you, but I do" He tries to keep his voice as steady as possible. "I hope you like.." He couldn't finish his sentence because she gave him a tight hug.

"Thank you so much" is all she said. He hugged her back and buried his face on her shoulder. He can feel her shuddering and start to sniffle.

"Heeey. Please don't cry. You're supposed to be happy. It's your day!" He let go of her hug and wiped the tears on her face. He holds her hand and smiles.

"No. You don't know how much that means to me. No one's ever really.. I never.." she looks down, unable to finish her sentence.

"Is that why you stay here until this late?" He holds her chin up so she can look at him.

She nods. "I just don't want to be alone on my apartment and realizing how lonely I am"

"You could've called me, you know" He tries to lighten up her mood, while actually his heart hurts to see her this sad. And thinking about how lonely she feels on her special day? She doesn't deserve that. She deserves all the happiness in the world! If only she knew how desperate he is to try to give her that happiness.

"Well, I was just afraid I might bother you. I thought you were on a date?"

"No, that was just an excuse because I had to go home early and bake you these" He pointed at the box.

"You baked these?" She takes the box and observes the cupcake one by one.

"You doubt my baking skill? Go on try it"

She takes one cupcake and gives it a bite. Her eyes light up as she tastes it.

"Told you" He smiled with pride.

"I didn't know you can bake?" she giggles.

"Are you kidding me?" He rolled his eyes. "There are a lot of things you don't know about me, Daice"

"Oh yeah? What else that I don't know about you?"

"Let me see" He pauses and watches her finish that one cupcake. She looks like a little kid. But he’s happy to see her that happy. Ever since he met her, it was clear that she had been through a lot of things, and he can see how much her past is eating her slowly day by day. He tries his best to take every opportunity to make her at least look a little alive. Even though it only lasts for a few minutes. "I never actually go on those dates that I told you about" He lowered his voice down and looked at the floor.

"I knew that already"

"What? How so?" He looks back at her and furrows his eyebrows.

"Well, you always have those twitches on the corner of your eyes when you lie."

He sighs. She is that good at reading people. Well, she does not have many friends. He shouldn't be surprised.

"I hate how easy you can read me" He pouted.

"Well, that is the only easy part. The rest is not really"

"Oh? I thought you knew everything?" He let out a chuckle.

"You.. you just.. unpredictable, Joe"

Is she... blushing? Even in the dim light of the Petal Paradise he can see her cheeks are turning into a slight pink.

"Well, I still win then" He grinned.

"Fine. You are" she smiles widely at him. His favorite smile of all people on this entire planet. The only smile that can warm his heart, and convince him that the world is not a scary place.

The only smile that makes him fall so hard for her.

"Well, there's another thing..." He said hesitantly.

"What other thing?" She looks at him curiously.

"That you don't know about me" He’s contemplating whether he should go tell this one to her or not.

"Oh? Okay what is it?"

Shit, okay there's no turning back now, isn't it? What else can he tell her that she hasn't known about him yet? Even with the previous fact, she can already guess it perfectly. He can't make up other stories because she would know that he’s about to lie again.

He means, he has been dying to tell her about this anyway. No matter what will happen, at least he has to tell her. Because he may never have the same opportunity anymore.

He take a deep breath before he shut his eyes and say "the I love you thing was not a joke"

He said that as quickly as possible. But there's only silence. He doesn't dare to open his eyes, because he can feel her staring deep at him.

"W... what?" After a few seconds that feels like eternity, she finally speaks with a really low voice, almost like a whisper.

"God damn it, Daisy," He chuckled nervously. "I was never joking when I said I love you" with all the courage he has left, he opens his eyes to stare back at her. "Ever since I met you. You helped me..."

"Hold your kite for it to fly just like any other kids’ kite? Yeah, I do remember. But.. how?"

He shrugged. "I don't know. You never really know when and how you fall in love with someone, right? You just.. did. And also the fact that you were always there for me. Even when I screwed up, or... having some existential crisis. Or whenever I feel like I want to just give up. You might never realized it, but you've done so much to me and that is what makes me fall even deeper for you"

She seems hesitant. "But.. I.. I'm not as good as you think I am.."

"Daisy" He cut her off because he knows that she's about to start the whole I don't deserve you thing. Because he knows that's what she's always doing. Thinking that she doesn't deserve to be loved, to be happy. "If you want to reject me please find a better excuse. I mean come on. That's lame. You don't have to say anything back. I was just letting you know something about me that you don't know, remember?" He tries to let out a laugh to stop himself from crying.

"Who said I want to reject you?" She takes his hands, and then tilt her head so she can look at him. "I never thought that you would feel that way to me because it's just impossible for a guy like you to fall for a girl like me. You're like a dream that could never be real."

He places his hand on her cheek and caresses it with his thumb. He leans his body in slowly and hugs her. He can hear her heart beating so fast as he closes his eyes and inhales her sweet scent. It feels like the world has stopped moving and the time stands still. He wants this moment to last forever. Just him and his childhood friend.

She immediately returned the hug and wrapped her hands around his neck. "This is the best birthday I've ever had, and you're the best gift I've ever got in my life"
