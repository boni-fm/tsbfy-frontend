Future Time

Yusuf Andika
Fantasy

In the time of 2057, the world changed drastically. Artificial intelligence has dominated the city population around the globe. Even most of the population are Android and humanoid robots. One of them is Bobby. He is the new generation of Humanoid AI that has been created by Frost industry, the company that produces all the humanoid robots.

Bobby is a robot that has a special attraction to history. He likes to learn about the past, and now he works as a teacher at a high school. Most of the students disrespected him as a teacher because they have no interest in history. But he does not care about it and keeps teaching.

He found a very dusty book during a visit to a library in the center of town. That book contains human history. He was so intrigued and wanted to read more details, but he found nothing when he searched it on the internet. Then, he tried to access the main database of Frost Industry to find anything which was related to it. Surprisingly, Bobby found out that the CEO of Frost Industry itself, Jack Frost, was the one who was behind all of the removed histories.

Inside that data, he also found the location of all humans who were still alive and exiled from the civilization. After that, he walked towards the main office of Frost Industry and tried to meet with Jack. At first, the security guards held him because of trying to enter without permission. But in the next hour, Jack reached out to Bobby by himself to talk. Bobby was questioning him about why he did that terrible action to mankind.

Jack answered without hesitation that humans could cause devastation and destruction to the world. When he was young, he saw so much destruction on the planet caused by humans. War, land destruction, and pollution are glimpses of what Jack sees and found out that the world would no longer exist if humans were still doing what they did. Because of that, Jack decided to move all mankind to a place that was half-destroyed because of them. He created all Artificial Intelligence to keep the rest of the planet in good condition. To separate the exile place and the good place, Jack built a gate and wall fully guarded by the robot army.

Bobby tried to give Jack the understatement that if humans were told about how to keep the planet safe, he believed that humans would understand and change their habits. He also showed the dust book of human history to Jack. Jack’s heart started to grow up and see the meaning of what Bobby said.

On the next day, Jack asked Bobby to accompany him to the exile place to see how mankind would survive. Jack was surprised to see the half-destroyed land become a paradise place for human lives. Jack feels guilty for judging them too fast and separating them from the place where they should be living. Since that day, Jack has broken the wall and let all the humans enter the city. He also picked Bobby to guide and teach them to keep their planet in good condition.
