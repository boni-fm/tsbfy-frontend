PROMISE

Keshatita
Romance

Love isn’t supposed to hurt. It isn’t supposed to be complex, nor unjust. Love is supposed to make someone feel comfortable and free. It should be liberating and wonderful. Either it comes from a simple hello, a soft stare, warm hugs, or words of affirmation from the ones we truly adore. Those simple actions can make anyone feels butterflies and make their whole day. With love, it eventually comes trust, loyalty, and commitment. But that basic foundation of love that keeps everything together like house of cards. You remove one and everything falls apart. The falling usually comes from issues, doubts, despair, uncertainty and many more. It shows a person where the true path is leading, or sometimes it is simply just a wrong signal.

   

    There once lived a couple living in an apartment in a quiet street of Brooklyn. They used to be madly in love with each other and decided to move in together. But after living for about 2 years, the spark was slowly gone. They don’t feel like sharing each other's lives and feelings anymore. The girl was getting a promotion and was asked to move to Copenhagen, Denmark. While the boy was having a very steady job in Manhattan and had no plans to move. They were discussing their plans for the future.

“So, what do you think?” The girl shot a question.

“Do you love this job?” The boy asked.

“Of course, I do.” The girl answered.

“I mean, we have everything here. I can afford us. You can stay here with me forever and perhaps, find another job here still. But if you do want this, I guess we can find a way.” The boy explained.

“Do you still love me?” The girl asked.

“What do you mean?” The boy looked confused.

“I asked if you still love me.” Stated the girl again.

“Of course I do, babe. What-“ The boy was caught off guard.

“Why do I get the feeling this ends with a goodbye?” Said the girl, softly.

“Why? What makes you think so? Do you still love me?” The boy asked sternly.

“I do too. But, I feel sad.”

“Don’t be. We will figure it out, love.”

“You know, maybe us moving out of here is a good idea.. I mean, getting new experiences and a fresh start.” Said the girl, unsurely.

“You don’t like Brooklyn anymore?”

“It's not that, it's just, maybe Copenhagen can be a great place for more opportunities. Plus, it's only us here.”

“I don’t know, babe. Living here was our dream, or at least mine.”

“I know. And I hope you know that I never want you to give up on yours, especially not because of me.”

The boy went silent. He was contemplating himself. For him, the future seemed to be right now. He loved his job. And even when it comes to their feelings, he still wants her to stay by his side because he knows that she is his comfort blanket, and if home is not a place then it would definitely be her. They will somehow figure everything out just like they always do, eventually.

“Hearing you say that makes me want to though.” He smiled softly,

They stared and continued reading each other

“Don’t.” The girl was about to cry.

On the contrary, the girl was so certain that they would be separated. One because there is no way she would let him sacrifice everything just for her and two because of what’s been going on lately as well. She felt the relationship was slowly drifting apart and that led her to believe that it was about time for goodbye, even though it might feel like hell. So when she heard him, she was caught off guard. She didn’t think that he would actually go with her at all and that made her sad. The girl had always been very independent and self-sufficient. Even after having him by her side, she knew that people were bound to change and leave anyway. She didn’t want to get attached and be too heartbroken like she used to be. She knew that the only person she can truly count on is herself. This wasn’t a wrong mindset at all in this generation. In fact, this was what the majority of young people would have when it comes to love and relationships nowadays. Too many issues and betrayal. We are all bound to make mistakes. But the question is, what are you willing to do to make it up? How far would you go? How much would you sacrifice? All these things remain uncertain. In the end, only action speaks.

“If you want to go to Copenhagen, I will go with you. Bear with me on this, but I don’t want to lose you. I would rather lose my job and everything I have except you. This was a hard decision for me, but I am willing to give it up because you deserve it.”

The girl broke down crying. She couldn’t believe his words.

“I wanted to make things right between us again. But I really don’t know how. Its either me staying or us separated. And I- I can’t think of staying, babe. I hate it here, I tried so hard to settle, but it’s been really hard. Plus, The headquarters is huge and Denmark is a nice country. I looked up everything-'' She continues to cry even more. “Are you sure about this? Please, the past month has been so blurry that I don’t know about our relationship anymore. It kills me. I don’t know what I did and I don’t know what you did to make us feel this way. It feels like we are breaking apart slowly and none of us know the cause of it. Especially now that this plan exists, it gives me a solution that I am not even sure if I wanted to take. I mean, I wanted to but without you- I would break.”

“I know it's been really hard between us, I know how much we’re struggling a lot, but I want to make it right. At first, I didn't think it was possible to move out of here and I thought everything was okay. Now that I know you didn't like it here, there is no reason for me not to help you be in a better place and I am going to be there too. In fact, why would I be here and if you weren't in it? What would my life be like if you weren't there from the beginning? You are God’s gift for me, for all things I went through, at least there’s you in it. You make me a better man. I finally realized that a home is not always a place, it could be a person too.” The boy smiled.

They hugged each other.

“You have no idea how much weight has been lifted off of my chest. I thought I’ll leave, I thought there’s nothing left for us, I thought everything that we built together has to end, I thought..”

He caressed her cheek and kissed it. He shook his head, “It won’t. I will make sure of it. You just have to trust me wholefully.”

“Thank you. You are the most wonderful and generous being anyone can truly have.” She expressed.

“I know. I am just so great.” He replied jokingly.

“I love you.” She said,

“I love you too. Always.” He said. “Promise to stay with me?”

“I promise.”

      The boy and the girl finally moved to Copenhagen together. The girl fulfilled her job there and the boy also got his job pretty quickly. They planned on settling there for as long as they both wanted. Apparently after staying there for a while, life is great again. It only takes a little bit of communication and a tiny magical telepathy for their love to bloom again. After all, it was just a false signal. That signal that can potentially break them apart. At last, it was sorted out and they have remained together ever since.
