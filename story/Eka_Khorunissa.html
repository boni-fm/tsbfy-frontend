Sunday Morning's Surprises

Eka Khoirunissa
Horror

Selina was a second-year high school student. Selina Dunfort, she was a cheerful student who enjoyed making jokes with her classmates. Not only that, but he was a standout student who was always the overall champion. She and her friends were eating lunch in their school cafeteria on that day. After selecting from a variety of food and drink choices, they proceeded to a seat that was still empty and quiet. Her pals were Elise, Conan, Anne, Robert, Lily, and Selina’s cousin, Sven.

        She felt someone was watching her during their lunch since she was still looking for a seat. Finally, Selina dared to stare at her and noticed someone she recognized watching her. That person was Gianna, her cousin's chair mate.

        She also gave a small smile because Selina felt she knew her, and it would be impolite not to say hello. Selina felt Gianna was still watching her until she and her friends finished their lunch. She ignored it and continued walking toward her class with her friends.

        The days following, Selina found out that someone was watching her every day. Earlier, she found a small razor blade while cleansing the desk drawer wherein she was sitting. Selina never brought sharp objects. She has touchy and skinny pores and skin, so she definitely avoids sharp items that might doubtlessly injure her. The other day, she observed a paper with bloodstains on her desk whilst nobody had come to class as it was still too early.

        "Is this just my feeling, or is someone playing a prank on me, huh?"  Selina talked to herself when she felt someone's prank for the second time.

        Selina didn't care at first because she thought these things were trivial. After the clock showed lunch break, Selina, as usual, went to the cafeteria with five of her friends. She chose the food menu and put the food on her tray.

        Gianna suddenly approached her at the cafeteria table, and she looked nervously and scared at her cousin, Sven. Gianna, Selina thought, had looked at her yesterday as if she were judging her.

        "I have something to tell you, Selina."

Selina responded with a stutter because she had never spoken to Gianna except for academic problems or things that required group work.

         "What's the matter, Gianna? Why do you appear to be scared or being pursued by someone?"

        Gianna turned away and hurried into their class, saying, "Sorry, I seem to have forgotten what I intended to say. Next time, Selina!"

Selina looked at Gianna's departure in confusion. Then Elise also spoke up to break the tension that was quite tense because of the expression Gianna gave earlier.  

        "Never mind, maybe Gianna just forgot to say something important, and because she forgot, she's scared of it."  

        "Yeah, that could be it. Let's finish our food because we're going to class soon."  

Sven added Elise's sentence so that Selina and the others rushed to finish their food. In her heart, Selina still felt something nagging because Gianna felt like she was about to say something really important, presumably from the gestures she made when she spoke to her.

        Starting from that incident, Selina often found that Gianna wanted to talk to her every chance. Strangely, every time Gianna seemed to want to approach Selina, at that moment, Gianna looked afraid of something. Until one day, Selina caught the look on her pale face for a moment when she was about to start a conversation.

        "Guys, have you noticed that Gianna's been weird lately? She often wants to talk to me, but on the other hand, when she's about to say something, she runs away and looks scared. Is my face so scary that it makes her nervous and scared?"

         Ronan replied with an astonished expression, "Yeah, Gianna's been watching our movements a lot lately, especially Selina. What exactly is she trying to say? I'm really curious. I swear."

        "I think I saw something. Two days ago, I was in the school parking lot. I saw Gianni talking to someone with her head down. I clearly remember that it was a male posture and voice. However, it was getting late, I ignored them and immediately rushed home. Later, I didn't ever ask Gianni anything because I felt maybe it was a personal thing."  Anne explained while looking at her friends who were in front of her.

        “That’s suspicious. If I were you, I would have asked this to Gianna.” Robert interrupted while Anne told him.

        Selina was even more curious about her friend's story. As for she was planning to follow behind the movements of Gianna. Because she felt that what Gianna wanted to tell her was something very important. Starting the next day, Selina carried out her mission to follow Gianna secretly. On the first day, she watched Gianna's movements. She realized that Gianna always had lunch in the cafeteria and chose to sit in the direction where her gaze was on Selina. The second thing that Selina realized was Gianna always visited the backyard of the school building, waiting for someone.

        “Is Gianna consuming drugs? I think it's currently booming among school students like me, but is Gianna like that? Gosh! I'm getting more and more curious about that!" Selina said while still observing Gianna, who was still waiting for someone.

“Selina, what are you doing here?” A voice that Selina believed was her cousin, Sven, startled her, who was hiding.

“God! Sven, for real.”

“What is it? Are you stalking a boy?”

“Sssstttt! Please lower your voice. I was watching Gianna.”

“What was that for?”

“Lately, Gianna always looks like she wants to talk to me, but she never says a word to me, and that makes me very curious. As a result, I plan to follow her so I can get a clue as to why she's acting weird like that.”

“Oh my God, Selina. We'd better go home right now. Come on, quickly!”

“Why? Is something wrong?”

Suddenly, from the direction where Gianna was standing, Selina saw Gianna screaming for mercy because she was being stabbed by someone she knew very well, Robert. Robert repeatedly stabbed the knife at Gianna without making any expression. He turned his head towards Selina and Sven.

“Do you guys want to feel it too? Alright, get ready because you guys are the next target,” said Robert, grinning at the two of them.

Robert took his knife on Gianna's drooping body and was about to attack them both. Selina and Sven turned around and ran as fast as they could. Selina's whole body shook, and her legs felt weak, and she wanted to cry. Meanwhile, Sven gripped Selina's hand tightly while pulled her hand tightly. But, somehow, Robert ran. He was able to catch up with Selina and Sven, and push them to the corner of the yard that would lead to the school classes.

“You can’t run anymore, Selina, Sven.”

“ROBERT, WAIT. WHY IN THE WORLD ARE YOU DOING THIS?” Selina asked Robert.

“You never knew what was going to happen, Selina. On a sunny day, it is not impossible that, ten minutes later, rain started to pour. That’s why you must get your umbrella.”

When the knife was about to hit Selina and Sven, Selina felt sucked into another dimension and spun like she was in a car that fell into a cliff. Selina screamed until she heard her alarm.

‘Beep-beep-beep’

Selina jumped from her bed with tired looks and pale face, cold sweat dripping from her forehead.

“For God's Sake, what is that?!” Selina was very shocked about the unexpected dream.

“Lina!!! Wake up, please, honey. Sven is waiting for you below.” Selina heard her mother's voice from the dining room.

        Selina took a bath in a hurry because the school gate was about to be closed in thirty minutes. After that, she went to school with Sven.

“Sven.”

“Yes? What happened, Selina? You look like a zombie because of your appearance.” Sven giggled to his cousin.

“I think I dreamed about something scary.”

Selina tells Sven about the dream to Sven from the start when Gianna looks suspicious and wants to talk about something, but she seems afraid.

“Selina, what if Robert is there with me?”

Sven seemed to hold a knife in his hand and grin at her.
