HIGHSCHOOL LOVE STORY

Yuliatri Theia Esterina
Romance

Ara, wait!" Lia shouted from the end of the corridor towards Ara. "Why are you walking so fast? My legs are shot." Lia said curtly. Seeing Ara's flat expression, Lia fell silent because she realized that something was wrong. "Ra, what happened? Is there anything wrong?" Lia asked. Ara still didn't pay attention to her words, but still walked hand in hand with Lia to their classroom. From the class bell ringing until the recess bell rang, Ara just stared out the window absentmindedly. Lia, who was her seatmate, could only stay silent and wait for Ara's response to her remarks earlier. Dinda and Yara, who are friends behind their bench, are also confused by Ara's attitude. Dinda poked Lia's back, then coded her 'what's wrong?' eye glance. Lia just shrugged her shoulders, pursed her lips down, and hinted 'don't know' to Dinda. Ara's three best friends could only stay silent and wonder about Ara's different behavior from usual days.

"What happened with Ara? She’s dumbfounded like a crazy person." Yara said to Dinda and Lia when the three of them were in the bathroom on the second floor. "I don't know, Yar. I've asked before but no response." Answered Lia. Seeing Lia's response, Yara and Dinda could only glance at them. Finally they approached Ara who had been alone in class to accompany her. "Ara, what happened? Tell us! We're so confused," said Dinda to Ara who only clasped her hands and glanced out the window. “Okta-” Ara opened her mouth, then closed it again. The three friends, who had been curious about waiting for Ara's answer, were finally annoyed and irritated. "What? Why Octa? Isn't he the one you have liked since 10th grade? What happened to him?” Yara said impatiently towards Ara. With a sad tone, like she wanted to cry, Ara answered "Okta and Elsa are now official."

Lia, Dinda, and Yara were shocked. "Huh, are you serious?! You’re joking right?" Lia said not believing Ara's words. Finally Ara's tears broke, but Dinda and Yara laughed out loud hearing this. "Why are you laughing? I liked Okta first, why did he end up with Elsa anyway?!” Ara said while sobbing. "No, it's nothing, but you're pretty dumb too." Dinda said laughing. "What do you mean!" Ara's reply was getting hotter. "Of course, ra. Do you think there's only Okta in this school? It's okay." Yara's response continued Dinda's words. "Relax first," said Lia, "Have you told Okta that you like him?" Lia asked. "Not yet." Ara replied, shaking her head as she continued to cry. “JEHHHH!!” Lia, Dinda, and Yara simultaneously responded to Ara's stupidity. "No wonder Okta shot his chance on Elsa, he doesn't even know that you like him." Yara said. "The rule is if you like one person, just say it! Don't bother trying to cover up everything." Connect Dinda. Ara sobbed while looking at the three of them. Her red eyes and snot flowing through Ara's nose made Lia, Dinda and Yara laughing out loud. "Guys, don't laugh!" Said Ara who was sobbing while laughing too.

"Eh ra, Okta is really handsome. But there are still MANY handsome people in this school. You're an idiot if you just cry for him." said Lia. Ara responded “But right-”, “No buts.” Reply to Dinda. After Yara gave a tissue to clean Ara's eyes and nose, Dinda suddenly said something unexpected. "I heard that there is an ipa child who likes you." Dinda said, teasing Ara. "Huh, I swear? Who isn't?!" Lia said curiously. "Yes, din, don't hang like that, like doi." Connect Yara. Lia answered with fiery eyes “His name is Dino, if I'm not mistaken, yes. He said it was our generation. But I don't know, that's what he said huh…” Dinda said, still teasing Ara. Ara, who had stopped crying, looked at Dinda with a sad-puppy facial expression and said, “Who? I don’t know him." Dinda was amused to see Ara's expression, "You are surely a pathetic human being, HAHAHA." Dinda laughing out loud. "Come on, just move on, ra." Lia said.

Suddenly someone knocked on the classroom door. The four of them looked in the direction where the sound came from. A tall, well-built, handsome-looking boy walked into the classroom and approached the four of them.

"Who are you looking for?" Lia asked.

"I want to meet Ara." Said The Boy.

Ara raised her right hand.

“Ara?"

"Yes"

"Hi, I’m Dino."

Immediately Dinda, Lia, and Yara gasped and looked at each other while smiling. Understanding the situation, the three of them immediately left Ara and Dino in the class, "Guys, let's go to the canteen first," said Lia. Ara's three friends walked out of the classroom smiling and whispering "Speak of the devil, my oh my."

Dino noticed Ara's red eyes and cheeks, "Are you crying?" Ask Dino.

"Yeah, why?" said Ara, who was too lazy to make small talk in response to Dino.

"I'm just saying, I've been watching you since the tenth grade." Dino said.

"And?" Ara chirps curtly.

"Yeah, I've been meaning to say this for a long time. Honestly, I really like you, ra."

Ara was silent for a moment. Many things crossed her mind, including how to answer a guy who had just confessed his feelings for her. The girl was silent because she never knew how to respond to the situation. "Anjir, what should I answer?" Ara said in a panic in her heart. He looked towards the classroom door, hoping that someone had come in, or that his friends had returned from the cafeteria. But the atmosphere was very quiet, not to mention Dino who was anxiously waiting for Ara's answer.

"GOSH, I FORGOT!" Ara shouted instantly, breaking the silence. Dino, who had been silent, was also surprised to hear the girl's cry.

"Uhm- Dino, Mrs. Asri told me to go to the teacher's room during break time. I'm so sorry, I'm in a hurry." Ara said as she stood up from her seat and pretended to panic out of the classroom. Dino was confused while smiling to himself seeing the cute behavior of the girl who ran away leaving him alone in that social studies class.

Downstairs, Ara frantically looks for three flocks of lambs (aka her best friends) who like to play out and leave as they please. As soon as she saw her three friends from afar, Ara shouted as loudly as possible “YOU ARE TRULY! HORRIBLE CHILDS!” Dinda was shocked, Yara spilled her drink, and Lia choked on the food she almost managed to swallow.

"Why did you get angry? You sound like a debt-collector!" Dinda snapped.

"Yeah, I spilled my drink!" Yara said, annoyed. While Lia was still coughing hitting her chest, hastily took a drink and took a sip.

"Don't be like that Ra! It hurts my throat." Said Lia who was still coughing.

"Btw, Din, you owe me a debt that hasn't been returned-" "Yes, come on Yar, I'll return it-" Dinda answered calming Yara.

"Guys, focus!" Ara said, cutting off the conversation of her three friends.

"Yes Ra, what happened? Eh- how was Dino?" Yara asked.

"Well there he is- how am I going to explain it?" Ara said who wanted to continue her sentence, but was silent.

The four of them were both silent waiting for each other. Annoyed that no one was speaking, Lia said, “WHAT ABOUT IT, ARA!? You are always cliffhanging me!"

"Why are you upset?"

"YOU- you just pissed me off now." Lia finished her sentence.

"What about Dino, ra?" Ask Dinda

"The guy said that he likes me." Ara replied in a flat tone.

"HAH?! Are you being serious?" Suddenly the three friends of Ara were shocked and stared in disbelief.

"Get it very quickly, you use implants, Ra." Yara chirps

"Hush, don't talk carelessly Yar." Lia said

"It means his love fortune is smooth." Dinda said

"Eh time? So how are we, Din?” Ask Lia

"You really are acutely single, stupid. There have been many who have shot their chances and got rejected." Dinda replied, annoyed with Lia.

"Yes, nothing is right, is it too late? How about you?" Reply Lia

"Still waiting, the best is always on time." Answer Dinda

"Bullshit, you're pretentious, Din." Yara's response refused to hear Dinda's words.

"Yeah right." Lia said.

Ara, who had been silent for a long time, only listened to her three best friends, who were even busy talking about their love story. "Hey, how about me? I'm panicking!"

"What did Dino say earlier?"

"Yeah, he just said, I've been watching you since the tenth grade. I've been wanting to say this for a long time. Honestly, I like you, ra." Ara said again Dino's words were the exact same thing that was said to him.

"Then you answer...?" asked Dinda excitedly.

"I said Mrs. Asri called me to the teacher's room, then I pretended to panic and ran out of the classroom." Ara answered innocently.

"Oh, it's fixed if this-" Lia responded to her friend.

"What fixed Li?" asked Ara who was still in her innocent tone.

“REALLY FIX IF YOU ARE A STEWPID!” Lia shouted in front of Ara's face. Instead of being angry, Ara laughed out loud when she saw the expression on her best friend's face, which was hot when she heard Ara's response. Dinda and Yara also laughed out loud at the cute way Lia spoke when she was angry. Lia was confused seeing her three friends laughing, finally she herself laughed too.

"Li, why do you seem to have a big grudge against Ara?" Dinda giggled.

"After that, I was really annoyed, '' Ara said half-heartedly, “how people don't really wait for her. '' Then it's annoying, Okta is crying, Dino is hung like that." Lia said.

"Yeah, how about Li, I don't know how to respond. If I refuse, I'm guilty, if I accept, I still have doubts." Answer Ara. A confused look appeared on Ara's face.

Yara, Dinda, and Lia understand very well that Ara is not a girl who is experienced in romantic relationships, let alone doesn't know what "dating" is.

"But do you like Dino?" Dinda asked Ara.

"He is cute. He’s really cute actually. He looks like a good guy, I guess." Digress Ara.

"Well then, what are you waiting for!" Yara exclaimed.

"Wait a second, think with your brain for once, won’t you? First, this is someone else's child, then if it doesn't fit the feeling, it is going to be complicated." Dinda's response to Yara.

"Well, it's better if you think about it first. Tell Dino if you need time to think." Lia gave advice to Ara in the midst of Dinda and Yara who were fighting.

"Sometimes your brain is working properly, Li." Ara said.

"Well, my brain is always working properly, unlike you who have a bug!" Lia responded to her friend's joke.

The bell resounded loudly throughout the school, signaling that the next lesson was about to begin. The four friends cleaned up the rest of their trash and immediately rushed to the north building, to their class. The rest of the time before the bell rang they spent gossiping and having fun, because the class was empty and the second teacher was absent because of illness.

After the bell rang, the four best friends were still in class. They deliberately took a long time to tidy up books and stationery because they still wanted to chat and have fun, and did not want to rush home. Unexpectedly, the boy who had approached Ara during lunch break came back to the class. Ara was impressed by the boy's actions. "I think he is loyal, well… because he came in here twice." Dinda whispered to Lia. While Yara just smiled as she looked at Ara, Dino, and her two friends in turn, waiting for what would happen.

Dinda felt that Ara would tell Dino that she was still in doubt and needed time to answer. Yara feels that Ara will reject Dino because she still likes Okta. Lia thought that at most Ara would find another reason to avoid.

"How about it?" asked Dino with a face full of hope.

"How about what?" Ara replied with a smile.

"The answer for the lunch-break question..." Dino said, giving a hint of the question Ara would answer.

"Well, I like you too. Let's date!" Ara replied casually.

"HAH?!" Dinda, Yara, and Lia shouted all together.
